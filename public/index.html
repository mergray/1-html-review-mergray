The Meredith Grey Story

A Triple Threat

Meredith Grey is a world-renowned general surgeon, wife to Mcdreamy, and mother of three.
She is currently the head of General Surgery at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital. Her husband, Derek
Shepard, was also head of neurosurgery at Grey Sloan Memorial. Meredith and Derek have three
children named Zola, Bailey, and Ellis. She manages to be successful and give time to both her
family and her career.


Testimonial

The most amazing thing about Meredith, though, is that she takes all that pain, all that loss, and
she turns into drive. Drive to save lives, to make things better. And despite all that she's lost,
she continues to find joy in her work as a surgeon, as a teacher, as a mother. And she managed to
share that joy through her spirit of discovery and of possibility and of hope, right in the face
of darkness.-Jackson Avery
